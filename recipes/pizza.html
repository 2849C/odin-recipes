Pizza



Description

This is a quick and basic cheese pizza recipe which requires store bought pizza dough.

Feel free to add whatever topping you like to it.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato puree
  • Cheese
  • Cornmeal
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 420 degrees F.
  2. Lightly spread cornmeal on bottom of pizza pan.
  3. Stretch out pizza dough to fit the size of the pan and gently lay in pan over cornmeal.
  4. Make crust by pinching or folding the edge of the pizza dough.
  5. Spread tomato puree lightly over the dough.
  6. Season to taste with garlic and onion powder.
  7. Spread desired ammount of cheese over the sauce.
  8. Lightly add salt and pepper over the cheese.
  9. Lightly spread olive oil over the cheese.
  10. Place on middle rack in oven and cook for about a half hour.
