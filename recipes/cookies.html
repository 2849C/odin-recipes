Cookies



Description

A simple chocolate chip cookie recipe with basic ingredients.

Great fresh or frozen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time then stir in vanilla.
  4. Mix baking soda into hot water and add to batter, along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour chocolate chips and nuts.
  6. Drop lange spoonfuls onto a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes or until edges are nicely browned
