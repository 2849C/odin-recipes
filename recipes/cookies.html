Cookies



Description

A simple chocolate chip cookie recipe with basic ingredients.

Great fresh or frozen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time then stir in vanilla.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda and chocolate chips.
  5. Roll into balls with hands and place on a baking pan with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until edges are browned.
