Pancakes



Description

A simple old fashioned, plain pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder salt and sugar together in a large bowl
  2. Add milk, egg and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Scoop and pour batter onto pan using a small ladle.
  5. Cook until browned on both sides.
